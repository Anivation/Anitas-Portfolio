2023 · UX Redesign

Scrapbays: A Seamless B2B Experience

I led the redesign of Scrapbays, a B2B marketplace for auto salvage. This included discovery, user research, design systems, and responsive interface design for both buyer and seller flows.

Discovery & Strategy

I facilitated remote stakeholder workshops to identify business challenges and conducted competitor analysis across UK-based platforms. User interviews highlighted key frustrations with legacy dashboards, speed, and lack of trust signals.

  • Conducted competitor UX audits (CarTakeBack, Copart, ASM Auto)
  • Mapped out seller and buyer journeys
  • Developed key personas: Fleet Seller, Trade Buyer

Design & Results

I created a new visual identity and component library in Figma, optimised for mobile-first use cases. The design included: contextual onboarding, real-time bidding interface, and clear listing standards.

  • Improved lead-to-listing ratio by 43%
  • Reduced drop-off at bid screen by 28%
  • Introduced trust signals (ratings, location map, seller profile)
