The Perfume Shop: Optimising PDP for Conversion

As UX & Design Lead at The Perfume Shop, I ran an A/B testing programme to enhance the product display page (PDP), collaborating with developers, analysts and our testing partner Evolv AI.

Tools & Insights

  • Used Contentsquare's Zoning Analysis to identify interaction hotspots
  • Gathered qualitative feedback from store staff on user confusion around pricing
  • Benchmarked industry patterns to inform UX design

Key Improvements

  • Repositioned flexible payment options above the fold (+5.57% uplift)
  • Introduced price strikethrough to address price perception (+3.77% uplift)
  • Made image thumbnails sticky and repositioned (+3.42% uplift)
  • Improved typography legibility and CTA clarity

Results

Over 5M sessions tested with 10 high-performing variant combinations exceeding control. The PDP uplift resulted in £160,000+ additional revenue in May 2025 alone.
