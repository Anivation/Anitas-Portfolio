Using ContentSquare’s Zoning Analysis, I confirmed that users were highly engaged with the ‘free delivery’ option which also correlated with strong conversion rates. This insight led me to reposition these options above the fold to increase visibility.

User research revealed that customers were confused about pricing differences between online and in-store promotions. This feedback inspired the introduction of a price-matching format online to provide greater price transparency and align expectations across channels.

Content Pages Redesign – Accessibility 2025

  • Improved Readability & Structure: Clear headings, increased font sizes, and better spacing enhance readability for all users—especially those with visual or cognitive impairments.
  • Accessible Interaction & Guidance: Key actions like ‘Start Chat’ are clearly labeled and visually prominent. Contextual prompts guide users who may feel unsure about how to engage.
  • Reduced Cognitive Load & Visual Clutter: Simplified layout and purposeful content reduce distractions, helping users—especially those with ADHD—complete tasks efficiently.
