WorkJoint: Bridging Hybrid Work Gaps

I redesigned WorkJoint, a job platform connecting flexible workers with short-term employers across retail, warehouse, and hospitality. The platform was in beta and needed stronger UX to onboard users and build trust.

Problem & Research

Feedback from early users revealed onboarding drop-off, unclear application flows, and poor conversion due to low trust. I ran competitor UX reviews (JobToday, Indeed Flex), and simplified job matching through clearer labels and filters.

  • Redesigned onboarding to include tiered verification
  • Added profile completion prompts to encourage repeat usage
  • Visual hierarchy adjustments to reduce cognitive load

Results

The improved flows resulted in a 37% increase in verified job seekers and a 25% increase in completed job applications. Employers also noted an improvement in candidate quality and fewer support queries.

  • 37% more verified job seekers (month-on-month)
  • 25% more successful application completions
  • 50% fewer help desk tickets about profile issues
